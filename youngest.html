Day in Life of Me: Youngest Brother


My youngest brother is six years younger than I. He is on the spectrum. I enjoy watching him try and process things.


I see my youngest brother playing video games on the TV. What should I do?


Ignore him and lay on the living room couch.
Mess with him. Block his view by standing in between him and the TV.
Tell him to sit down. He's standing far too close to the TV. It's gonna hurt his eyes.
Hug him from behind. This little dude hasn't talked to me all day.
How I interact with my goofy younger brother.
