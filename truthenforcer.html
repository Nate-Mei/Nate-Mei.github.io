One of Most Recent Armor Sets

The Truth Enforcer armor sets have probably one of the best armor perks in this game: Unflinching. This armor perk allows you to aim down your gun without the stagger of enemy attacks, meaning you're basically unbothered by any minor inconvenience. I personally love this perk as it helps me a lot with dealing with pesky enemies with no worries about missing my shots. Also it just looks awesome.
